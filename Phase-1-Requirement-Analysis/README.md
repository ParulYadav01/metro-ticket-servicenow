Business Objectives:
Digitize metro ticket booking using ServiceNow for fast, accessible, and automated ticket generation via QR codes.

Enhance Commuter Convenience 

Implement user-friendly digital ticketing options such as mobile apps, QR codes, and WhatsApp-based bookings to reduce wait times and streamline the ticketing process.

Increase Operational Efficiency

Adopt Automated Fare Collection (AFC) systems to minimize manual interventions, reduce errors, and lower operational costs.

Promote Digital Payment Adoption

Encourage the use of digital payment methods like UPI, credit/debit cards, and mobile wallets to facilitate cashless transactions and improve revenue collection.

Facilitate Data-Driven Decision Making

Utilize data analytics from digital transactions to gain insights into passenger behavior, peak usage times, and route optimization opportunities.

Support Environmental Sustainability

Reduce paper usage by transitioning to digital tickets, contributing to eco-friendly practices and aligning with sustainability goals.


Functional Scope:
Service Catalog item for ticket booking with variables like Source, Destination, Passenger Type, Number of Tickets.

Automated fare calculation, QR code generation of digital tickets.

Stakeholder Mapping:
Passengers (end users), Station Managers, Metro Operations Team, IT Admins.


Execution Roadmap:
Milestones: Catalog Form Setup → Fare Logic Script → QR Code Integration → Station Details Mapping→ UI Pages →UAT & Deployment
